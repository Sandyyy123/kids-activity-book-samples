# Kids National Park Adventure Passport

**120 pages | Ages 8-12 | U.S. National Parks + Major Landmarks**

A passport-style activity and journal book for young adventurers. Kids collect stamps, learn facts, answer challenges, and record their own adventure plans for 30+ U.S. National Parks.

## Book Structure

- **Introduction + How to Use Your Passport** (6 pages)
- **Park Entries x 30** (3 pages each = 90 pages)
- **Major Landmarks Bonus Section** (12 pages)
- **Master Adventure Bucket List** (6 pages)
- **My Explorer Profile + Back Matter** (6 pages)

## Each Park Entry Contains

1. Park stamp header (name, state, year established)
2. Fast Facts grid (size, famous feature, wildlife, cool fact)
3. Did You Know? (1 surprising fact)
4. Adventure Prompts (3 open-ended creative questions)
5. My Journal (lined writing space)
6. Bucket List Challenges (6 activity checkboxes)
